Electronic Access & Discovery Librarian

June 2022 - PRESENT
Birmingham, AL

In my current role at UAB Libraries, I provide technical support to a diverse user base across five campus library locations, including medical and historical libraries. I use tools such as Oracle, vendor platforms (SUSHI, COUNTER5 harvesting), Tableau, Google Analytics, and Excel. These insights help inform decision-making and improve user experience. I also utilize my knowledge of Bootstrap 5, CSS, HTML, JavaScript, and AngularJS to enhance the functionality and accessibility of Primo VE and LibGuides. I ensure that all enhancements comply with ADA accessibility standards to support an inclusive user environment.

My work aligns closely with the strategic goals and service mission of the university and library. I contribute to digital collections and facilitate resource sharing through collaborative efforts with peer institutions, including participation in initiatives such as the Digital Library of the Middle East (DLME).
